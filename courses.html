Meet Your trainer: Arjun Thakur


Arjun Thakur is the founder and chief instructor at Tech Pro School, an EdTech company offering corporate technical training, interview preparation services, AWS certification training, and courses on software engineering and computer science.


Arjun has fourteen years of experience working for big tech companies like Amazon and startups across the globe, including India, the USA, and Southeast Asia.


Arjun has been on the other side of the interview table and has interviewed hundreds of software engineers for big technology companies, including FAANG/MAANG.


Arjun is a strong advocate of loosely coupled, highly scalable software systems, adhering to the best practices of software engineering.
